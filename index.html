About Me

Hello! I'm Aditya Verma, a passionate and detail-oriented developer specializing in creating modern, responsive web applications. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js), I strive to build engaging user experiences and efficient backend systems. I have worked on various personal and collaborative projects that showcase my ability to blend creative design with logical structure.

My journey began with curiosity about how websites work, which gradually grew into a professional pursuit. I love solving real-world problems through code and constantly stay updated with the latest trends in frontend and backend development. I'm a self-motivated learner who enjoys challenging tasks and continuously seeks to improve and expand my skill set.

Outside coding, I have a keen interest in UI/UX design, performance optimization, and accessibility standards. My goal is not just to develop functioning applications but to ensure they are also user-friendly, fast, and accessible to all users.

I believe in clean, maintainable code and efficient workflows. Whether it's integrating APIs, managing databases, or designing from scratch, I approach every task with the same level of dedication and professionalism.

I’m always open to collaborating on exciting projects or discussing new ideas. Feel free to connect with me!
